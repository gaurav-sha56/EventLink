Ignitia: A Cultural Fest Extravaganza!

Event Details: Ignitia

Ignitia is an annual cultural fest, a vibrant celebration of art, talent, and creativity! Get ready for a spectacular showcase of dance, music, drama, literary arts, and much more. It's an opportunity to connect, compete, and create unforgettable memories.

Organizers: Cultural Committee, Tech University

Event Head: Dr. Ananya Sharma

Co-Founder: Mr. Rohan Kapoor

Date: October 26-28, 2025

Venue: University Grounds

Theme: Fusion of Traditions

About Ignitia

Ignitia aims to foster a spirit of camaraderie and healthy competition among participants. It's a platform for students from various colleges to display their skills, learn from each other, and experience the rich cultural tapestry of our nation. Expect dazzling performances, engaging workshops, and exciting competitions across various genres.

Ignitia Innovation Challenge

This innovation challenge, part of Ignitia, is aimed at promoting problem-solving and creative thinking among college students. Teams from various colleges are invited to participate.

Each team must consist of *2-4 members. The event will be conducted over **48 hours*, and prizes will be awarded to the top teams.
